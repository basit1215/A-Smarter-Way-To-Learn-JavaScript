Chapter#11 Comparison operators

  • When you're comparing strings, the equality operator is case-sensitive. "Rose" does not equal "rose."

  • Comparison operator, "===" is equal to & check data type.

  • Comparison operator, "!==" is not equal to & check data type.

  • Comparison operators, ">" is greater than.

  • Comparison operators, "<" is less than.

  • Comparison operators, ">=" is greater than or equal to.

  • Comparison operators, "<=" is less than or equal to.
  • Comparison operator, "===" is only equal to.

  • Comparison operator, "!==" is only not equal to.
